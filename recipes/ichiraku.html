Ichiraku Ramen

Description

Naruto's favorite meal from Ichiraku Ramen! Ramen is a classic example of Japanese comfort food with its delicious broth and soft noodles. Being a miso soup-base tonkatsu ramen with pork chashu, this is a meal meant for a hero.

Ingredients

Chashu Pork
  • 2 lbs pork belly
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 mirin
  • 3/4 cup sake
  • 2 tbsp sugar
Miso Ramen
  • 2 tbsp sesame oil
  • 2 garlic cloves
  • 1 tsp minced ginger
  • 1/4 lbs ground pork
  • 1/2 cup miso paste
  • 8 cups chicken stock
  • 1 tsp soy sauce
  • 1 cup chashu braising liquid
  • 12 oz fresh ramen noodles
Toppings
  • Chashu pork slices
  • 1 soft boiled egg
  • Thinly sliced green onion
  • Narutomaki fish cake slices
  • Bamboo shoots
  • Nori seaweed

Steps

Chashu Pork
  1. Preheat oven to 290F.
  2. Combine the soy sause, mirin, sake, and sugar.
  3. Place the pork belly in a baking dish and poor the mixture over.
  4. Cover the baking dish with foil.
  5. Place the pork in the oven and cook for 2 hours or until the internal temperature of the pork reaches 195F.
  6. Remove the pork from the oven to cool.
Miso Ramen Soup
  1. Mince the garlic and ginger.
  2. In a medium pot, heat the sesame oil over medium-low heat and add the minced garlic and ginger.
  3. Stir fry until fragrant then add ground pork.
  4. Once the ground pork is cooked, add the miso paste and sugar. Mix well.
  5. Add chicken stock and soy sauce.
  6. Add chashu braising liquid
  7. Keep at a simmer while you cook the noodles.
Ramen Noodles
  1. Bring a medium pot of water to a boil.
  2. Cook the noodles for about 15 seconds or according to package instructions.
  3. Take the noodles out with a mesh sieve and shake out the excess water.
Combining everything!
  1. Place the noodles in a bowl.
  2. Add the ramen soup.
  3. Place on all the toppings and serve immediately.
